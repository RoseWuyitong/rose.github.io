About Me.

Hey there! I'm Yitong Wu, you can also call me Rose.

I'm A dedicated and driven professional in the field of financial technology. With a Master's degree in Financial Technology and a Bachelor's degree in Mathematics with Economics, I possess a strong foundation in both quantitative analysis and technological expertise.

As a Data Engineer at OCBC Bank, I've mastered trade comparing automation, tackled complex data challenges and implemented solutions with precision and accuracy, ensuring efficient and streamlined processes. At Singtel, I dabbled in Data Science, unravelling customer behaviour and leveraging PySpark to drive insights.

Beyond the tech realm, I'm fluent in English, Mandarin (native), and even Japanese (JLPT N2). 一緒に会話しましょう!

Outside of these, I engage in various activities that foster personal growth and balance. I enjoy solving Sudoku puzzles to sharpen my analytical skills, (trying to...) maintain a disciplined fitness regimen by lifting heavy weights, and exploring my creativity through the art of making bread!

If you are searching for a versatile professional with a passion for fintech, strong language skills, and a penchant for innovative thinking, I would be delighted to connect with you. Together, we can navigate new frontiers, unravel complexities, and foster a mutually beneficial partnership.
